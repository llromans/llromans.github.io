Start to plan what your website will look like, make a wireframe (simple design and sketching of what each page should seem like) and a sitemap (a list of what the pages are how they connect and the order of them)

Also think what will this site have to do, will it need to process a payment, will it need to have a shopping system (cart, proceed to payment, etc.), also choose what you will make it with, (HTML and CSS for this guide, but you could use a website software or something.
